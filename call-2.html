…and I hung up the phone. Immediately I recollected the voice that had spoken in German.

It was that of Captain Richard Madden.

Madden, in Viktor Runeberg's office, meant the end of all our work and - though this seemed a secondary matter, or should have seemed so to me - of our lives also. Before the sun set on this same day, I ran the same risk.

I went up to my bedroom. Absurd though the gesture was, I closed and locked the door. I threw myself down on my narrow iron bed, and waited on my back… It seemed incredible that this day, a day without warnings or omens,

might be that of my implacable death.
